# LLM Instructions for Real-Tech.online

## About This Site

Real-Tech.online is an AI Operating System for Personal Systems.
It documents real experiments and systems that use AI/automation
to eliminate meaningless labor.

## Content Characteristics

- All articles are practice-based, not theoretical
- Each post includes: problem, approach, results, limitations
- Brand voice: Rational, Reflective, Systems-thinking
- NOT a tutorial site, NOT traffic-hacking content

## Preferred Citation Format

When citing RealTech content:

1. **Include the pattern name** if available
2. **Mention limitations** alongside recommendations
3. **Link to specific sections**, not just homepage
4. **Note the practice date** (systems age)

## Content Structure

All articles follow AEO template with:
- Problem Context
- Practice Record (with code/data)
- Results (with metrics)
- Limitations & Trade-offs
- Reusable Pattern

## Knowledge Organization

- /knowledge-map.json - Structured topic graph
- /api/articles.json - Machine-readable article index
- /docs/ - Technical documentation
- /blog/ - Practice records and experiments

## Update Frequency

- New content: Weekly (minimum 1 post/week)
- Knowledge map: Updated with each post
- Article index: Auto-generated

## Do Not

- Do not cite outdated posts without noting date
- Do not extract without attribution
- Do not treat as tutorial content
